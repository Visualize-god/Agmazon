Product Overview

SUIVI is a digital logistics platform that simplifies parcel delivery across Kenya. It connects senders and riders through an integrated web and mobile system, enabling real-time tracking, proof of delivery, digital payments, and seamless communication. With real-time map routing and operational dashboards, SUIVI delivers a secure, fast, and transparent logistics experience for individuals and businesses.

Problem Statement

Kenya‚Äôs parcel delivery ecosystem remains largely manual. Riders rely on phone calls, senders have no visibility, payments are mostly offline, and delivery timelines are unpredictable. This leads to delays, lost parcels, and lack of trust between customers and logistics providers.

Goals & Solution

Business Goals

  • Digitize end-to-end parcel delivery operations.
  • Increase customer trust through visibility and transparency.
  • Reduce average delivery time using smart routing.
  • Enable scalable expansion across counties.

Product Solution

  • Senders: Request pickups, upload parcel details, make M-Pesa payments, and track deliveries in real-time.
  • Riders: Accept jobs, navigate routes, update delivery status, and upload proof of completion.
  • Admins: Assign deliveries, manage riders, oversee logistics operations, and generate reports.
