Product Overview

AGMAZON is an agricultural eCommerce platform designed to bridge the gap between Nigerian farmers and consumers. It connects producers and buyers via a seamless online experience, supporting everything from product listing to payment and delivery. AGMAZON provides transparency, ease of access, and market expansion for local agricultural businesses.

Problem Statement

Nigeria's agricultural market is fragmented, with farmers struggling to access reliable markets, fair pricing, and consistent delivery options.

Goals & Solution

Business Goals

  • Digitize the agricultural marketplace in Nigeria.
  • Empower local farmers and buyers with access and tools.
  • Facilitate secure payments and trusted delivery systems.
  • Build a scalable platform that supports regional expansion.

Product Solution

  • Buyers can browse, order, and pay securely online.
  • Farmers can list produce, receive orders, and schedule pickups.
  • Admins manage the marketplace, logistics, and support.

User Persona

1. Sade – Cassava Farmer

Needs: A reliable way to sell produce without middlemen.

Frustrations: Delayed payments and price uncertainty.

2. Ugo – Bulk Buyer

Needs: Affordable and consistent agricultural supply.

Frustrations: Manual negotiation and delivery tracking issues.

3. Admin (Platform Operator)

Needs: Manage users, transactions, and logistics efficiently.

User Flow & Customer Journey Map

Buyer Journey:

  • Browse Products → Add to Cart → Make Payment → Track Order → Receive Goods

Farmer Journey:

  • List Produce → Confirm Orders → Schedule Pickup → Get Paid

Admin Journey:

  • Monitor Marketplace → Support Users → Manage Delivery Partners → Oversee Transactions
