Product Overview

SUIVI is a digital logistics platform designed to make parcel deliveries across Kenya easier and safer...

Problem Statement

In Kenya, delivery remains manual and unreliable, with delayed parcel delivery, poor rider coordination, and offline payments that increase the risk of fraud.

Goals & Solution

Business Goals: Digitize delivery, improve trust and speed, scale across counties.

Product Solution: A 3-role platform for senders, riders, and admins, including tracking, payment, and dashboard features.

User Flow & Personas

Wanjiku (Sender), Otieno (Rider), Admin (Ops Lead)

Journey Maps

Sender: Request → Pay → Track → Feedback
Rider: Accept → Navigate → Update Status → Complete
Admin: Monitor → Assign → Manage → Report

Success Metrics

Delivery success rates, M-Pesa usage, app installs, support resolution time, etc.

My Role / Tasks

I wrote the PRD, defined user roles, and worked with engineers to implement features.

Key Results

Live MVP on Flask, tested notification flows.

Key Learnings

Transparency builds trust. Structure enables scale. Unified design improves adoption.
